#  Our Privacy Statement: SecureVault

## 1. Why SecureVault Exists
We created **SecureVault** as a simple, powerful way to show how digital privacy should work. In a world where every "Delete" button usually leaves a hidden copy behind, we wanted to build something different. This project is a proof-of-concept for **short-term, safe messaging**—where your words only exist as long as you are talking.

---

## 2. Our Security Promise
We follow a **"Zero-Footprint"** rule. This means we’ve designed the code to be as forgetful as possible for your safety:

* **Temporary Keys:** Every time you open the app, it creates a brand-new secret key. This key lives only in your computer's "short-term memory" (RAM).
* **No Paper Trail:** We never save your keys, your messages, or even the scrambled "gibberish" to your hard drive. Once the power goes out or the app closes, that data is gone forever.
* **Total Clean-Up:** As soon as you hit "Exit" or close the window, the key is wiped. Even if someone stole your computer a minute later, they couldn't recover what you said because the "map" to unlock the message no longer exists.

---

## 3. What This Is (And Isn't)
SecureVault is an **educational tool** built for:
* Students who want to see how **AES Encryption** (bank-level security) works in real life.
* Coders who want to learn how to keep data out of the hard drive.
* Anyone curious about how to send a truly "private" note.

**Note:** Since this app is built for speed and temporary safety, it doesn't "save" chats for you to read later. If you close it, the chat is gone—and that’s exactly how we planned it.

---

## 4. How We Protect You
We don't just rely on math; we rely on smart habits:
1.  **Tamper Alerts:** If someone tries to change even one letter of your encrypted message, the app will realize it’s been messed with and refuse to open it.
2.  **Screen Privacy:** The app clears your screen automatically so no one can look over your shoulder and read what you typed a few minutes ago.

---

## 5. Final Word
Security is a balance between strong math and smart memory. SecureVault proves that you can have a private conversation without leaving a trail of files behind. It’s simple, it’s fast, and most importantly, it’s built to forget.
